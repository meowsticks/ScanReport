Scan Locations · Findings & Recommendations

Core A · P1 Bay 1 mid-span 0.9 m × 0.9 m · 80 mm Ø
Observation. Clear cone at the proposed centre. Top mat at ~45 mm cover, bottom mat at ~210 mm. No tendons within scan window. Nearest top bar 65 mm S of centre — adequate clearance.
Safe  Cleared to drill as marked. Maintain 50 mm clearance from top bar.
Core B · P1 Bay 1 column line 0.9 m × 0.9 m · 80 mm Ø
Observation. A post-tension tendon crosses on a diagonal at ~130 mm depth within 30 mm of the proposed centre. Top mat reinforcement also dense at this location (#5 @ 150 mm o/c).
Caution  Relocate the core 80 mm south and re-scan before drilling. Revised mark applied on-site.
Core C · P1 Bay 2 mid-bay 0.6 m × 0.6 m · 60 mm Ø
Observation. Clean window between top bars; bottom mat at 220 mm. No anomalies in scan volume.
Safe  Cleared to drill as marked.
Core D · P1 Bay 2 wall pier 0.6 m × 0.6 m · 60 mm Ø
Observation. Top mat at 40 mm cover, regular spacing. No targets between top and bottom mat at centre.
Safe  Cleared to drill as marked.
Core E · P1 Bay 3 mid-span 0.9 m × 0.9 m · 100 mm Ø
Observation. Two post-tension tendons run NE–SW directly through the proposed core. Tendons identified by characteristic hyperbolic returns at 120 mm and 140 mm depth. Relocation by < 250 mm in any direction does not clear both tendons within the 100 mm core diameter.
No-go  Do not core at marked location. A 600 mm shift NW is required — proposed revised location E′ marked on-site for review with the structural engineer prior to drilling.

Areas of Uncertainty

Bay 4 NE corner. Faint anomaly at ~90 mm depth could not be classified to a confidence sufficient for clearance. Does not coincide with a planned core; cautious approach recommended if scope expands to include this area.
Bay 3 SW edge. Slab thickness was not independently verified at this edge — assumed 250 mm per drawings. Depths in this zone may shift by ±15 mm if actual thickness differs.

Limitations

  1. Depths derived from assumed dielectric constant (ε = 6.5); actual depths may vary ±10%.
  2. The 2.7 GHz antenna's effective depth is approximately 600 mm; targets below this depth were not assessed.
  3. The T-R offset creates a 0–58 mm near-surface resolution zone; shallow targets were verified visually where possible.
  4. GPR interpretation is inherently subjective; results reflect best technical judgment at the time of scan.
  5. Findings are not a substitute for as-built drawings or destructive verification (daylighting).
  6. This report is valid only for the scan area and date specified. Subsequent modifications void findings.
